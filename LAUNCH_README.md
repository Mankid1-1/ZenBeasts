### 3. The Core Loop (What to Test)

Please try to complete this sequence:

1.  **Mint a Beast**:
    -   Go to the "Mint" section.
    -   Click "Mint Beast" (Cost: ~0.05 SOL).
    -   Wait for the transaction to confirm.
    -   Verify your new Beast appears in "My Collection".

2.  **Perform Activity**:
    -   Select your Beast.
    -   Click "Perform Activity".
    -   Confirm the transaction.
    -   Note: You will earn "Pending Rewards".

3.  **Claim Rewards**:
    -   Wait a few seconds/minutes.
    -   Click "Claim Rewards".
    -   Verify your ZEN token balance increases in your wallet.

4.  **Upgrade Trait**:
    -   Select a trait to upgrade (e.g., "Strength").
    -   Click "Upgrade".
    -   Confirm transaction (Requires ZEN tokens).
    -   Verify the trait value increases.

5.  **Breed (The Ultimate Goal)**:
    -   You need **two** Beasts to breed. Mint a second one if needed.
    -   Go to the "Breeding" section.
    -   Select two parents.
    -   Click "Breed".
    -   Verify a new "Child" Beast is created.
