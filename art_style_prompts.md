# Art Style & Prompt Guide

This document contains sample prompts for generating beast images in various art styles.

## Low Poly Vector Style

This style combines the clean, sharp aesthetic of vector illustration with the geometric, faceted look of low-polygon 3D models. It's characterized by solid colors, sharp edges, and a minimalist feel.

### Core Style Prompt Modifiers

`low poly vector art`, `geometric style`, `faceted`, `flat shading`, `cel shading`, `minimalist 3D`, `solid colors`, `sharp edges`, `clean lines`

### Trait-to-Visual Mappings

*   **Strength**: `muscular build`, `powerful`, `large`, `sturdy`, `thick limbs`, `armored plates`, `broad shoulders`
*   **Agility**: `sleek`, `slender`, `streamlined body`, `long limbs`, `agile stance`, `aerodynamic`, `light frame`
*   **Wisdom**: `glowing eyes`, `intricate geometric patterns`, `ethereal aura`, `wise expression`, `runic symbols`, `floating crystals`
*   **Vitality**: `vibrant colors`, `healthy glow`, `bioluminescent markings`, `energetic pose`, `crystal-like horns or claws`

### Rarity-to-Visual Mappings

*   **Common**: Base design with trait influences.
*   **Uncommon**: `subtle glowing highlights`, `simple ornate markings`.
*   **Rare**: `pronounced glowing elements`, `faint elemental aura (e.g., fire, ice, electricity)`, `more complex geometric patterns`.
*   **Epic**: `dynamic elemental effects`, `swirling energy`, `floating geometric shapes`, `partial translucency`.
*   **Legendary**: `powerful radiant aura`, `celestial body patterns`, `cosmic energy`, `crown of light`, `transcendent form`.

### Sample Prompts

**1. Common Beast (High Strength, High Vitality)**
> "Low poly vector art of a powerful beast. A sturdy, quad-pedal creature with a muscular build and thick, armored plates on its back. The beast is depicted in an energetic pose, with vibrant, saturated colors and subtle bioluminescent markings on its legs, suggesting high strength and vitality. Full-body portrait, minimalist style, plain background, geometric, faceted, solid colors, sharp edges."

**2. Uncommon Beast (High Agility, High Wisdom)**
> "Low poly vector art of an agile beast. A sleek, slender creature with a lightweight frame and long limbs, poised for quick movement. It has wise, glowing blue eyes and simple, ornate geometric patterns on its fur. Full-body portrait, minimalist style, plain background, cel shading, sharp lines."

**3. Rare Beast (Balanced Traits)**
> "Low poly vector art of a rare beast. A majestic four-legged creature with a balanced physique. It has a faint elemental aura of crackling blue electricity around its claws and more complex geometric patterns across its body. Portrait shot, minimalist 3D, plain background, faceted, solid colors."

**4. Epic Beast (High Wisdom, High Agility)**
> "Low poly vector art of an epic beast. An ethereal, agile creature with a streamlined body that seems to float just above the ground. It is surrounded by swirling cosmic energy and small, floating geometric crystals. Parts of its body are translucent, revealing a glowing core within. Full-body shot, dark background to emphasize the glow, geometric, faceted style."

**5. Legendary Beast (High Strength, High Wisdom)**
> "Low poly vector art of a legendary beast of immense power. A massive, muscular creature with a wise, ancient expression. Its body is covered in celestial patterns that pulse with a soft, internal light, and it wears a crown of glowing, geometric light shards. A powerful, radiant aura surrounds it. Full-body portrait, cosmic background, minimalist 3D, faceted, sharp edges."

## Neon Cyberpunk Style

This style is defined by high-tech, futuristic designs with a focus on neon lighting and cybernetic enhancements. It can be split into two main aesthetics: a dark, gritty noir style and a bright, vibrant pop-art style.

### Trait-to-Visual Mappings (Cyberpunk)

*   **Strength**: `heavy cybernetic limbs`, `reinforced chassis`, `powerful pistons`, `industrial design`, `glowing power cables`
*   **Agility**: `sleek chrome plating`, `lightweight carbon fiber frame`, `aerodynamic fins`, `glowing energy conduits`, `hover jets`
*   **Wisdom**: `advanced optical sensors`, `holographic data displays`, `neural interface ports`, `intricate glowing circuitry`, `psionic energy crown`
*   **Vitality**: `polished chrome finish`, `bright LED patterns`, `stable energy core`, `pristine condition`, `bioluminescent data streams`

### Rarity-to-Visual Mappings (Cyberpunk)

*   **Common**: Basic cybernetic parts, simple LED lights.
*   **Uncommon**: `custom chrome plating`, `subtle holographic projections`.
*   **Rare**: `advanced cybernetics`, `visible energy flowing through conduits`, `dynamic holographic effects`.
*   **Epic**: `weaponized limbs (e.g., laser claws, energy cannons)`, `reality-glitching effects`, `body partially made of hard light`.
*   **Legendary**: `form composed of pure data and light`, `reality-distorting aura`, `constantly shifting form`, `controls weather and local data networks`.

### A. Dark & Gritty Cyberpunk

This sub-style is inspired by classic sci-fi noir like *Blade Runner*. It features dark, atmospheric settings, high-contrast lighting, and a feeling of urban decay.

**Core Style Prompt Modifiers**: `dark cyberpunk aesthetic`, `neon noir`, `dramatic lighting`, `rain-slicked streets`, `gritty`, `dystopian`, `photorealistic`, `cinematic`, `high contrast`, `glowing neon signs`

**Sample Prompts**

**1. Common Beast (High Strength)**
> "Photorealistic, dark cyberpunk art of a cybernetic beast. A powerful, heavily-built creature with industrial, heavy cybernetic limbs and exposed, glowing power cables. It stands in a dark, rain-slicked alleyway, illuminated by the high-contrast glow of a red neon sign. Full-body shot, gritty texture, cinematic, dramatic lighting."

**2. Rare Beast (High Agility, High Wisdom)**
> "Photorealistic, dark cyberpunk art of a rare cybernetic beast. A sleek, agile creature with advanced chrome plating and glowing blue optical sensors for eyes. Visible energy flows through semi-transparent conduits in its limbs. It is perched on a rooftop overlooking a dystopian cityscape at night, with dynamic holographic data streams projecting from its eyes. Full-body shot, neon noir aesthetic."

**3. Legendary Beast (High Wisdom)**
> "Photorealistic, dark cyberpunk art of a legendary A.I. beast. A transcendent being whose form is composed of pure, glitching data and shimmering hard light. It phases in and out of reality in a dark, server-filled room, its presence distorting the data streams around it. Its form is vaguely beast-like but constantly shifting. Cinematic, high contrast, volumetric lighting, god-rays."

### B. Bright & Vibrant Cyberpunk

This sub-style is inspired by modern, high-energy cyberpunk like *Cyberpunk: Edgerunners*. It features bright, saturated colors, clean designs, and a pop-art feel.

**Core Style Prompt Modifiers**: `bright cyberpunk`, `vibrant`, `saturated colors`, `pop-art inspired`, `clean lines`, `digital art`, `sleek`, `holographic`, `glowing`, `high energy`, `style of Ilya Kuvshinov and Studio Trigger`

**Sample Prompts**

**1. Common Beast (High Vitality)**
> "Bright cyberpunk digital art of a cybernetic beast. A creature in pristine condition with a polished chrome finish and dynamic, colorful LED patterns running across its body. It stands in a bright, futuristic city street filled with holographic advertisements. Full-body portrait, vibrant saturated colors, clean lines, pop-art inspired."

**2. Epic Beast (High Strength, High Agility)**
> "Bright cyberpunk digital art of an epic beast. A sleek and powerful creature with a lightweight carbon fiber frame and aerodynamic fins, but also heavily armed with glowing plasma cannons on its shoulders. It is captured mid-air in a dynamic, high-energy pose, with glowing energy conduits flaring. Sleek, pop-art style, vibrant colors, style of Studio Trigger."

**3. Legendary Beast (High Vitality, High Wisdom)**
> "Bright cyberpunk digital art of a legendary beast. Its body is made of shimmering, rainbow-colored hard light, with intricate, glowing circuitry visible within. It floats in the center of a futuristic city, projecting massive, beautiful holographic data displays into the sky. It has a transcendent, god-like presence. Full-body shot, vibrant, saturated colors, high energy, clean anime aesthetic."
